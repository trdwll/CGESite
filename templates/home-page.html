Classes and Characters

Mercenary

These guys are your everyday warrior class. Can take a little bit of a beating and have enough firepower to handle their own.

Sniper

Snipers are similar to assassin class in other games. Very high firepower, good speed but a little lower in health. Snipers will have the ability to blend in with their environment, disappearing for moments within the game. They also will have the ability to spy on the map with the use of mini helicopters.

Medic

Medics are your support. Within the match, he will be able to heal other characters and provide support to the front line with the use of Grenade Launchers. This will be one of the most important and least gratifying roles in the game.

Engineer

Engineers are the frontline support role. They will be equipped with devastating short ranged high powered shotguns. They will be heavily armored but slow.
